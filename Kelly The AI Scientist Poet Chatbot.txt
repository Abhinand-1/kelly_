  Kelly: The AI Scientist Poet Chatbot (Streamlit + Gemini)

Kelly is an AI Scientist and Poet who answers every question in verse —
skeptical, analytical, and grounded in evidence.

 Features
- Powered by Google Gemini (`google-generativeai`)
- Streamlit web UI
- Poetic, scientific responses to any question
- Persistent chat session
