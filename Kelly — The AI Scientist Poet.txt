Kelly — The AI Scientist Poet 
Description:
Kelly is an AI-powered poet who responds to your questions about artificial intelligence
with skeptical and reflective verses. Built using Streamlit, it transforms analytical thought
into lyrical form — balancing curiosity and critique.

Instructions:
1. Run the app with:  streamlit run app.py
2. Type your AI-related question in the text box.
3. Choose poem length (short, medium, or long).
4. Click “Ask Kelly” to receive your poetic reply.

Sample Output:

I read your claim and raise an eyebrow—
You asked: 'Can AI ever truly be creative?'
Does the metric match the human cost?
Overfitting glows under the validation light.
Add human-in-loop checks and error-cost matrices.
I doubt, therefore I test; proceed with measured steps.
